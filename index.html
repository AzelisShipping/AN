Implementation Plan

Weekly Actions

  • Monitor Castrol and other premium brand pricing on Amazon, focusing on best-selling SKUs
  • Utilize dynamic pricing tools (e.g., Amazon repricer) and adjust prices within ±5% based on demand and competitor moves
  • Track conversion rates and Buy Box win rates for all products

Monthly Review

  • Analyze market share changes in the premium synthetic oil segment
  • Review customer feedback on price-value perception
  • Assess promotional performance and adjust bundle strategies
  • Monitor margin performance against a target of ~25%

Quarterly Strategy

  • For 1L products:
    • Implement bundle deals (e.g., 3+1 free) during peak seasons
    • Offer Prime member exclusive discounts
    • Roll out new customer acquisition offers
  • For 5L products:
    • Offer volume discounts for purchases of 2+ units
    • Promote a Subscribe & Save program
    • Align seasonal promotions with service intervals
  • For 20L products:
    • Implement tiered pricing for commercial customers
    • Set free shipping thresholds for bulk orders
    • Introduce loyalty rewards for repeat purchases
