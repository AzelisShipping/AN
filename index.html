Implementation Plan

Weekly Actions

  • Monitor competitor pricing across all product categories and track any changes in their pricing strategy
  • Track sales volume for each product to identify any immediate impacts from price changes
  • Make dynamic price adjustments up to 10% higher or lower than the recommended price based on market conditions and sales performance (e.g., if a product is selling well, we can test slightly higher prices within this range)

Monthly Review

  • Analyze our market share position for each product category compared to key competitors like Castrol
  • Review customer feedback and complaints specifically related to pricing to identify any potential issues
  • Evaluate the performance of current promotions by measuring sales lift, revenue impact, and customer adoption rates

Quarterly Strategy

  • Run targeted promotions for 5L products during peak seasons (e.g., 10% discount when buying 2 or more units)
  • Create value-add bundle deals for 1L products (e.g., buy 3 get 1 free) to encourage bulk purchases
  • Implement tiered volume discounts for 20L products (e.g., 5% off for 3+ units, 10% off for 5+ units)
  • Conduct comprehensive review of pricing strategy effectiveness, including:
    • Analysis of revenue growth vs targets
    • Assessment of market share changes
    • Review of customer retention metrics
    • Evaluation of promotion effectiveness

Key Performance Indicators

Sales Metrics

  • Track monthly revenue against 12% growth target
  • Monitor volume changes by product with 5% growth benchmark
  • Compare market share against top 3 competitors

Price Position

  • Weekly tracking of competitor price movements
  • Monthly analysis of price sensitivity by product
  • Measure ROI on all active promotions

Customer Metrics

  • Monthly customer satisfaction surveys
  • Track customer retention rate (target: 85%)
  • Monitor brand perception scores
