Implementation Timeline

Phase 1: Data Analysis & Setup (Month 1-2)

  • Set up Excel pricing models and templates
  • Create initial Power BI dashboards
  • Document current pricing processes
  • Establish baseline metrics

Phase 2: UK Market Implementation (Month 3-4)

  • Implement standardized pricing workflows
  • Set up regular reporting cycles
  • Establish review processes
  • Train team on new procedures

Phase 3: Process Refinement (Month 5-6)

  • Optimize reporting dashboards
  • Refine pricing models based on learnings
  • Document best practices
  • Develop expansion templates

Phase 4: Market Expansion Planning (Month 6+)

  • Analyze UK market success metrics
  • Adapt models for EU markets
  • Create market-specific reporting templates
  • Develop expansion roadmap
